# MPPT Solar Charge Controller

*Industrial MPPT using Perturb & Observe (P&O) Algorithm*  
*Registered Invention Patent – University of Tamanrasset (2023)*

A practical and efficient Maximum Power Point Tracking controller designed to maximize energy harvest from solar photovoltaic systems in industrial and off-grid applications.

## Key Features
- Implementation of classic *Perturb & Observe (P&O)* MPPT algorithm
- Real-time voltage and current monitoring
- Optimized power tracking efficiency
- Designed for integration with DC-DC converters
- Strong focus on reliability and performance in harsh environments

## Project Highlights
- Officially registered as an *Invention Patent* at the University of Tamanrasset, Algeria.
- Demonstrates solid skills in power electronics, embedded systems, control algorithms, and renewable energy integration.
- Relevant to Electrical Engineering roles involving power systems, diagnostics, and field operations.

## Technologies
